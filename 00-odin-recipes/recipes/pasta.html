Pasta

Description

Pasta is a traditional Italian cuisine made from durum wheat dough that is shaped into various forms and cooked by boiling.

It serves as a versatile base for numerous dishes, often combined with sauces, meats, vegetables, and cheeses.

  • Pasta (spaghetti, penne, fusilli, or any preferred type)
  • Olive oil or butter
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Cherry tomatoes or canned tomatoes

Step for making pasta

  1. Bring a large pot of water to a boil.
  2. Add a generous amount of salt to the boiling water.
  3. Add the pasta to the boiling water and stir occasionally to prevent sticking.
  4. Cook the pasta according to the package instructions until al dente.
  5. While the pasta is cooking, heat olive oil or butter in a pan over medium heat.
  6. Add minced garlic and chopped onion to the pan and sauté until fragrant and translucent.
  7. If using, add cherry tomatoes or crushed tomatoes to the pan and cook until softened.
  8. Season the sauce with salt, pepper, and herbs like basil and parsley.
  9. Optional: Add spinach or other greens to the sauce and cook until wilted.
  10. Drain the cooked pasta and add it to the sauce in the pan.
  11. Toss the pasta with the sauce to coat evenly.
  12. Optional: Add cooked protein such as chicken, shrimp, or tofu to the pasta.
  13. Serve the pasta hot, garnished with grated Parmesan cheese and a sprinkle of fresh herbs.
  14. Optional: Add a squeeze of lemon juice or zest for added brightness.
